How It Works

The system follows one clean path, every time:

  1. Bring content

    Author in Word or Markdown, or import an existing course.

  2. Convert

    The engine structures it into one standard internal format.

  3. Brand & render

    TeleTracking colors, fonts, logo, and interactive blocks, applied automatically.

  4. Package

    Out comes a standards-based course file, ready to upload and track.

One Build, Every Format

Because the content is stored once in a neutral format, the same course can be published to whatever standard our platform needs — with no rework.

OutputUse
SCORM 1.2Broadest LMS compatibility
SCORM 2004Richer tracking and sequencing
cmi5 / xAPIModern learning analytics
Author once. Publish to any standard. Re-brand or re-localize without rebuilding from scratch.

Under the hood

The engine is a compact Python standard-library system — no servers, no GPUs. It reads Word or Markdown, converts to a neutral course format, applies a swappable brand profile, and emits SCORM 1.2, SCORM 2004, or cmi5. It runs air-gapped; the AI layer only assists authoring.
