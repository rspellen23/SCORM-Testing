How It Works

Three steps from idea to LMS-ready course.

No authoring tool. No design software. No back-and-forth with an agency.

1

Write

Draft your course content using one of our plain-language templates. Fill in the topic, the objectives, and the key points — no coding required.

2

Build

CourseCraft packages your script into a branded, interactive HTML course — complete with knowledge checks and completion tracking — in seconds.

3

Upload

Drop the zip file into your LMS. Done. Completion, pass/fail, and time-on-course are all reported back automatically.
