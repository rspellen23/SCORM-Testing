The draft is segmented into 10–15-min units, each grounded in adult-learning design. The AI chooses from the full block range as the content calls for it — knowledge checks, drag-to-sort / categorize, flashcards, accordions, process & timeline, comparison panels, infographics, charts (from real sourced numbers only), images, and callouts — with optional entrance animations.
